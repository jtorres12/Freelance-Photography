Who I Am

Manuel Espejel was born in 1991 in Chicago, IL, in 2016 he received his Associates in Arts for Graphic Design and is currently finishing his bachelor’s degree At Northern Illinois University with an emphasis on photography. Espejel has already been included in various important exhibitions including “A New Nothing” at Gallery 215, Dekalb, IL and “Blick Arts Materials Ars Nova Exhibition” at Gallery 215, Dekalb, IL. This year he will continue to attend NIU with finishing the degree this fall. Upcoming show this August include “BFA Senior Show” at Jack Olson Gallery, Dekalb, IL. Espejel currently lives and works in Aurora, IL.
